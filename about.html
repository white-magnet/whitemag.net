About us


White Magnet Pvt Ltd is a company operating from Hyderabad, India. We are a product and services company which builds software for bandwidth optimization and acceleration. We provide consulting and technical services to ISPs and small to medium scale network owners to manage proxy servers. Our team consists of self-motivated people graduated from the top technology school in India. We also undertake the assignment of customizing our software to fit the customer's networking environment. Delivering the products and services of high quality is our motto.
